Mohamed is Director of AI for Cisco's Contact Center solutions. In 2019, Cisco acquired Voicea, where Mohamed was a Co-Founder and Chief Architect; he also worked on ASR, NLP, and Voicea's AI assistant (EVA).
At LinkedIn and Microsoft, Mohamed led AI and big data teams that served hundreds of millions of users. He worked on LinkedIn's news-feed personalization, Outlook.com, and Visual Studio Team Foundation Server.
Mohamed co-authored a book, Computing with Data, that expounds how to work with big data. He's also been a Teaching Assistant for Machine Learning (CS229) and Deep Learning (CS230) at Stanford University since Fall 2019.
Mohamed is an avid calligrapher and photographer; in 2011, one of his shots made it as Bing.com's wallpaper; a few more got published in books after that. Before COVID-19, Mohamed loved to travel.
